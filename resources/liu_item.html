Beware of scam ads!

The Notice Board removes postings that doesn't follow our rules. Although, it happens that notices from scammers are posted. So be careful and make no money transfers until you have seen the apartment and a contract is signed.

To keep in mind:

  • Do not pay deposit or make any money transfers before you have seen the apartment and signed a contract/rental agreement
  • If the lessor requests payment via Western Union, it is likely to be a scammer.
  • If the lessor is located abroad and demands advance payments, it is likely to be a scammer.
